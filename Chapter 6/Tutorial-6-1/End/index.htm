A Trip to Remember

After returning from my three-month travel adventure, I found myself bitter and depressed. Fortunately, all it took was a little HTML wizardry to build this website commemorating my journey!

Now, anytime I miss my life-altering odyssey, all I need to do is bask in the blinking artificial light of my 14" computer monitor and, in the few spare moments of free time I have between soul-crushing data entry work at the chronically understaffed dot-com where I work, enjoy my carefully positioned and distinctly un-pixelated images.

I hope you enjoy these pictures as much as I do.
